Hotels

Greece offers an incredible variety of hotels, each capturing the country’s charm, hospitality, and unique blend of history and natural beauty. From luxurious seaside resorts overlooking turquoise waters to boutique hotels tucked into the winding streets of traditional villages, the accommodation options are as diverse as Greece’s landscapes. Whether you're dreaming of a cliffside suite in Santorini, a beachfront retreat in Crete, or a cozy guesthouse in the mountainous north, every region provides stays that reflect local character and warm Greek philoxenia—hospitality from the heart.

Across the country, hotels seamlessly blend modern comfort with cultural authenticity. Many feature architecture inspired by centuries-old traditions, while offering contemporary amenities such as infinity pools, spa facilities, and fine dining with Mediterranean flavors. Travelers can choose between family-run establishments known for personal service, romantic hideaways perfect for couples, or full-service resorts designed for relaxation and adventure.

Hotel Grande Bretagne

Located in Athens’ historic Syntagma Square, the Hotel Grande Bretagne is one of Greece’s most iconic and prestigious hotels. Since opening its doors in 1874, this landmark property has set the standard for luxury hospitality, offering guests an extraordinary blend of classical elegance, modern comfort, and unrivaled views of the city’s most treasured monuments.

Inside, the hotel exudes old-world charm with its ornate chandeliers, marble interiors, and richly decorated lounges that reflect over a century of refined Athenian style. Its rooms and suites combine sophisticated décor with contemporary amenities, many featuring private balconies that overlook the Acropolis, the Parliament building, and the vibrant city streets below.

Guests can indulge in world-class dining at the hotel’s renowned restaurants, including the GB Roof Garden, offering exceptional Mediterranean cuisine with sweeping views of the illuminated Acropolis. The hotel also features an award-winning spa, indoor and outdoor pools, and personalized concierge services that elevate every stay.

Four Seasons in Athens

The Four Seasons Astir Palace Hotel Athens brings world-class luxury to the stunning Athenian Riviera. Situated on the pine-dotted Vouliagmeni peninsula, this waterfront resort blends sophisticated modern design with the natural beauty of the Saronic Gulf, offering guests a serene escape just minutes from the energy of Athens.

The resort features two distinct experiences: Nafsika, with glamorous sea-view rooms and vibrant social areas, and Arion, offering refined elegance, tranquil suites, and signature Four Seasons serenity. No matter where you stay, you’re treated to sweeping views, soft private beaches, and the unmatched comfort the brand is known for.

Guests can enjoy crystal-clear swimming coves, luxurious pools, and an award-winning spa inspired by Greek wellness traditions. With over ten exceptional dining options—from upscale Mediterranean cuisine to world-class seafood—the resort creates unforgettable culinary experiences in scenic, oceanfront settings.

Cavo Tagoo in Mykonos

Cavo Tagoo Mykonos is one of the island’s most iconic luxury hotels, known for its striking minimalist design, dramatic cliffside setting, and world-famous infinity pool overlooking the Aegean Sea. Blending Cycladic architecture with modern elegance, the hotel creates an atmosphere of pure indulgence, where every detail is curated for style, comfort, and unforgettable views.

Guests can relax in spacious suites featuring private pools, cave-style jacuzzis, or terraces that open directly to sweeping sunsets. The hotel’s signature infinity pool—lined with glowing lights and floating sunbeds—offers a glamorous, effortless place to unwind, while the VIP-exclusive spa provides soothing treatments inspired by Greek wellness traditions.

Dining at Cavo Tagoo is an experience in itself, with its celebrated restaurant serving refined Mediterranean and Asian-influenced dishes against a backdrop of golden sunsets and shimmering waters. Located just minutes from Mykonos Town, the hotel offers easy access to world-renowned nightlife, boutique shopping, and charming Cycladic streets.

Cavo Tagoo is perfect for travelers seeking luxury, romance, and the ultimate Mykonos lifestyle—where every moment feels cinematic, and every view looks postcard-perfect.
